The questions in this quiz are taken directly from Umberto Eco's essay on "Ur-Fascism", which is widely regarded as the definitive guide to fascist thought. While a fascist need not hit all the points, it's not as strict an ideology like socialism or capitalism, the more points one hits, the more likely they are to be one.
